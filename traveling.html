Traveling


I love to travel, and have been fortunate enough in my personal and professional life to have seen so much of the country. From TDYs in Hawaii and Mississippi, to personal vacations in Alaska and Puerto Rico, to the splendor of my own home state of Utah. I'm very fortunate to have incredible hiking trails just out my front door, and frequently travel to nearby lakes and streams to fish. I've been to 20 states and dearly hope to visit all of the remaining 30; I would also very much like to visit all of the other US territories. Despite all my domestic travel, I've never left the country! Hopefully I can change that in the near future, with a lifetime goal of visiting every continent. Some of my bucket-list countries are Ireland, New Zealand, South Korea, Norway, The Philippines, Germany, and Kenya.
