Gardening


There are few things in life that I find more soothing than tending to my garden, both indoor and out. It is incredibly grounding to sink your fingers into bare earth (no pun intended). Most of my indoor plants are cacti and succulents, as it's nowhere near humid enough in my home to keep most other houseplants happy. But outdoors is where I really find my zen, cultivating a healthy garden of beautiful native perennials that are well-adapted to my hot, dry climate in Utah. From rocky mountain penstemon, to fragrant hummingbird mint, to nigh-unkillable sedums and opuntias.
